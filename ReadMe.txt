This is a typically race game.
When  you overtaking a car your score increase and when you touch a car you die and start again.
At the begining player's car is in the rigth bottum of the road and enemy's car is in the left top of the road.

There are 1 cpp and 6 header files:

ClassCar.h - describe class car
ClassRout.h - describe class route
EnumDirection.h - describe enum direction
CarFunctions.h - describe class car functionss
RoutFunctions.h - descibe class ruote functions
DescribeDirection.h - in this header are functions which get keyboards keys to move the car
